About 247 Performance

Where Elite Athletes Are Made

Our Mission

At 247 Performance Lab, we're committed to revolutionizing baseball training through cutting-edge technology, data-driven insights, and expert coaching. Our mission is to help every athlete reach their full potential, whether they're aspiring to play at the collegiate level or competing professionally.

We believe in a holistic approach to player development that combines biomechanics, strength training, mental conditioning, and sport-specific skills to create complete athletes.

Mission Image

Our Story

Founded by former professional baseball players and performance scientists, 247 Performance Lab was born from a simple idea: athletes deserve access to the same world-class training tools and expertise used at the highest levels of the sport.

What started as a single facility has grown into a nationwide network of performance labs, each equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by passionate coaches who are dedicated to athlete development.
